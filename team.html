Meet the GingerSec Team

Helping make business faster, more efficient, and more secure.

Tom Tardy, MS, C|EH, C|HFI

CEO, Founder

Tom founded GingerSec after working in the InfoSec industry for several years. Prior to working in InfoSec, he spent 25 years in law enforcement. During that time he worked various assignments including detectives, training, and patrol. He also investigated white-collar crimes and different types of computer crimes. Tom is passionate about developing new InfoSec professionals to help address the shortage of qualified individuals. He works to mentor prior public safety professionals to prepare them for a new career in InfoSec.

Mallory Clark

Chief Operations Officer

Mallory Clark is the Chief Operating Officer for GingerSec. She holds 4 associates degrees with distinction in mass communications, marketing, behavioral health, and juvenile corrections. Mallory oversees our daily Operations at GingerSec, as well Bid Management. Mallory also specializes in social media marketing. She handles social media accounts with upwards of 30,000 followers to include media with over 7million views. She has vast knowledge over multiple social media platforms and brings a unique perspective to the GingerSec team. Though her main job as the chief operating officer is of the utmost importance, Mallory enjoys working with the marketing team to help grow GingerSec.

Danielle Doty

Chief Creative Officer

Danielle oversees all things creative for GingerSec. From branding, to UI/UX design, to social media and marketing Danielle is excited to help GingerSec customers establish their digital footprint in a way that is authentic and unique to them. With a background in full stack web development and business analysis Danielle is ready to meet any business need our customers may have. As a CompTIA certified technical trainer Danielle helps to train the security professionals of the future.

Andrew Crotty

Chief Security Officer

Andrew Crotty holds a Bachelor of Science in Criminal Justice with a certificate in homeland security from Arizona State University. He has been working in investigations and reports for over 10 years with 5 years as a police officer with the City of Phoenix which he currently holds the title of Detective. Andrew is also in the US Army Reserve as an All-source Intelligence analyst which helps thorough deep dive investigations with attention to detail.

Andrew Humphrey

Chief Technical Officer

Andrew Humphrey is known for providing excellent customer service and his ability to help individuals understand and use technology to its fullest extent. Andrew currently provides project management for GingerSec as well as technical support for many different IT services. Andrew has been in IT services the past 6 years. He began with an A+ certification installing and repairing systems. He has expanded his work to systems administrator, most recently, for a company with 70 users, at eight locations, providing onsite and remote support 24/7. He enjoys building computers and server systems and repairing laptops and most electronic/ computer equipment.

Noah Tardy

Chief Field Officer

Noah Tardy is the Lead Manager for all Construction jobs for GingerSec, LLC and the Chief Field Officer. Noah controls all of our field operations and runs the team from the ground. Noah supervises field techs and their day to day jobs. Noah specializes in cabling, server rack installs, camera installation, and access points. Noah also build custom PC's and hardware builds.
